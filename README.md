# 🛍️ Customer Shopping Behavior: Consumer Insights & Subscription Analysis

## 👋 Overview

This project explores the shopping patterns of **3,900 customers** across diverse product categories (Accessories, Clothing, Footwear, and Outerwear). The goal was to identify the key factors—such as demographics, shipping preferences, and discounts—that drive high-value purchases and customer subscriptions. By analyzing transaction history and review ratings, I provided actionable insights to improve customer retention and maximize revenue per segment.

## 🚀 Key Features & Analysis

  * **Customer Segmentation:** Classified customers into age groups (Young Adult, Middle-aged, Adult, Senior) to identify high-spending demographics.
  * **Subscription Behavior:** Analyzed the profile of "Subscribed" vs. "Non-Subscribed" customers to identify opportunities for conversion.
  * **Category Performance:** Evaluated revenue contribution across Clothing, Accessories, and Footwear to determine product-market fit.
  * **Review & Rating Correlation:** Investigated how average review ratings impact customer loyalty and frequency of purchase.
  * **Operational Analysis:** Studied the impact of shipping types (Express, Free, 2-Day) on total purchase amounts and customer satisfaction.

## 🛠️ Data Toolkit & Methodology

  * **Data Preparation (Python):** Cleaned raw transactional data, handled missing review ratings, and performed feature engineering using **Pandas**.
  * **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA):** Leveraged **Matplotlib** and **Seaborn** to visualize distribution by gender, age, and location.
  * **Revenue Modeling (SQL/Excel):** Calculated total revenue by category and derived customer-level metrics like `average_purchase_amount`.
  * **Visual Intelligence:** Developed an interactive **Power BI Dashboard** featuring real-time slicers for gender, subscription status, and category.

## ⚙️ How It Works

1.  **Phase 1: Data Sanitization** – Cleaned the 3,900-row dataset, addressing missing values in the Review Rating column.
2.  **Phase 2: Segment Analysis** – Used Python to group customers by age and calculated revenue contribution per group.
3.  **Phase 3: Subscription Profiling** – Created a comparison model to see if "Repeat Buyers" are more likely to opt into premium subscriptions.
4.  **Phase 4: Dashboard Deployment** – Visualized KPIs like Average Review Rating (3.75) and Average Purchase Amount ($59.76) in Power BI.

## 📈 Key Business Insights

  * **The Subscription Gap:** Found that while only 27% of customers are subscribed, they account for a significant portion of repeat purchase frequency.
  * **Top Revenue Drivers:** The "Clothing" category emerged as the primary revenue leader, followed closely by Accessories.
  * **Age Group Impact:** "Young Adults" were identified as the highest revenue-contributing segment ($62k+), suggesting a need for youth-focused marketing.
  * **Shipping Loyalty:** Customers using "Next Day Air" and "Express" shipping tended to have higher review ratings, indicating that speed is a key driver of satisfaction.
